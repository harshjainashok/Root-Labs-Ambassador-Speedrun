Fragile Stage: Agreement Signed → First Post Live
The problem: Root Labs ships free product the moment an ambassador signs — then hopes they post. 56 of 60 ambassadors took the product and never posted. That's not an ambassador program, it's a gifting program with no accountability.

The fix: Flip the product trigger. No product ships until the ambassador submits a content draft for review. The agreement includes a 7-day posting deadline. Product ships same-day once the draft is approved. This changes the sequence from "product first, post optional" to "commitment first, product second." Expected impact: activation rate from 6.7% to 70%+.
