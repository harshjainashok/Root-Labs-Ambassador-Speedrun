The 4-Message Recovery Sequence

1
The Check-In Day 1
Goal: Re-open the conversation without pressure. Gauge if they even tried the product.
Hey [name] — been a few weeks since we sent the gummies over. Curious if you've had a chance to try them? No pitch, genuinely want to know if they worked for you. A lot of people notice the energy shift around day 5-7. 43 words
Why it works: Asks a question they can answer without committing to anything. "No pitch" disarms the guilt of not having posted.
2
The Content Shortcut Day 4 — if they replied
Goal: Remove the "I don't know what to post" friction. Give them a content idea they can shoot in 5 minutes.
Glad you liked them! Quick idea if you ever want to share it — a 15-second morning routine clip. Just the gummies next to your coffee or pre-workout. No script needed. Some of our creators just film their actual morning and it outperforms the polished stuff. 45 words
Why it works: "If you ever want to" keeps it low pressure. The content idea is so simple it removes creative paralysis.
3
The Social Proof Nudge Day 8 — if no post yet
Goal: Show them others are posting and earning. Create gentle FOMO without guilt.
Thought you'd want to see this — [creator name] posted a quick Story review last week and drove $380 in sales from it. She earned $57 in commission from one Story. If you're open to it, I can send over her exact format. Takes about 3 minutes. 48 words
Why it works: Real dollar amounts make commission tangible. Offering a template reduces effort to near-zero.
4
The Graceful Close Day 14 — final message
Goal: Close the loop cleanly. No bridge burned. If they're not going to post, free up the slot.
Hey [name] — totally understand if the timing isn't right. I'll close out your ambassador slot for now so there's no hanging obligation. If you want to jump back in later, just DM me. The product is yours to keep regardless. Thanks for giving it a try. 48 words
Why it works: "Close out your slot" creates scarcity. "Yours to keep regardless" maintains goodwill. Some people re-engage only when they feel the door closing.
